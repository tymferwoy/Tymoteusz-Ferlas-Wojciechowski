Reboot – Focus App

Reboot is a minimalistic focus tool that helps users reduce digital distractions by limiting access to selected applications during user-initiated focus sessions. Built using Apple's official Screen Time APIs.

About the App

Reboot is designed to help individuals stay focused by blocking distracting apps during focus periods the user chooses. Reboot uses Apple's Screen Time APIs (FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, ManagedSettings) and requires explicit permission from the user. All focus behavior is entirely user-initiated.

The app gives users full control over their digital environment, allowing them to start focus sessions with a simple NFC card scan. No tracking, no spying, no monitoring – just a simple tool to help you stay focused.

Features

NFC Card Control

Start and stop focus sessions by scanning your physical Reboot NFC card. A tangible commitment to focus.

User-Initiated Sessions

You control when focus sessions start and end. No automatic monitoring or forced restrictions.

App Selection

Choose exactly which apps to limit using Apple's Family Activity Picker interface.

Focus Statistics

Track your focus sessions, build streaks, and see your productivity progress over time.

Privacy First

No device usage data collection, no tracking, no analytics. Your focus sessions stay private.

Screen Time API

Built using Apple's official FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, and ManagedSettings frameworks.

How It Works

1

Select Apps

Choose which apps you want to block during focus sessions using Apple's secure app picker.

2

Scan Your Card

Hold your Reboot NFC card near your iPhone to start a focus session.

3

Stay Focused

Selected apps are blocked. When you're done, scan your card again to end the session.
