Reboot – Focus App

Reboot is a minimalistic focus tool that helps users reduce digital distractions by limiting access to selected applications during user-initiated focus sessions or custom schedules. Built using Apple's official Screen Time APIs.

About the App

Reboot is designed to help individuals stay focused by blocking distracting apps during focus periods the user chooses. Reboot uses Apple's Screen Time APIs (FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, ManagedSettings) and requires explicit permission from the user. All focus behavior is entirely user-initiated.

The app gives users full control over their digital environment, allowing them to create custom schedules and manage which applications they want to limit during focus sessions. No tracking, spying, or monitoring takes place.

Features

User-Initiated Focus Sessions

Start and stop focus sessions whenever you need them. Complete control in your hands.

Custom Schedules

Create personalized focus schedules that match your daily routine and productivity goals.

App Selection

Choose exactly which apps to limit using Apple's Family Activity Picker interface.

Screen Time API Integration

Built using Apple's official FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, and ManagedSettings frameworks.

Privacy First

No device usage data collection, no tracking, no analytics. Your focus sessions stay on your device.

No Data Sharing

All permissions are requested through Apple's official APIs. Nothing leaves your device without your control.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Reboot handles your data.

Data Collection

Reboot does NOT collect Screen Time data. We do NOT collect device usage data. We only store user-created schedules and session durations that you explicitly create.

Data Storage

Schedule data is stored using Firebase Firestore solely for account synchronization and schedule backup. All data is user-controlled and can be deleted at any time.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with third parties. Your focus sessions, schedules, and app selections remain private.

Analytics and Tracking

No analytics are collected without explicit user consent. We do not track your behavior or monitor your device activity.

Screen Time Permissions

Reboot uses Apple's Screen Time APIs (FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, ManagedSettings) with explicit user permission. These permissions are required for the app to function and are requested through Apple's official authorization flows.

User Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app settings.
